Todd Giles – banjo and vocals

Todd Giles is a clawhammer banjo player from Massachusetts with oldtime musical roots. Former banjo player and singer in Boston based ‘Poverty Line Old Time Band’, a band that lived by the motto “more banjo less pants.” Todd moved to The Hague in 2010, and played Banjo alongside Mike Bailey and Jan Den Boer in former Hague based outfit ‘The Cannonball String Band’ as well as in 'The Bootlegger's Trial'.
