Jan den Boer – double bass

Hague born double bass player Jan den Boer has many of his musical roots in oldtime blues. He developed himself as a session musician, and has played in many different bands within the Hague and further afield, notably the Little Boogie Boy Blues Band, with whom he has toured all over Europe. Most recently Jan was bassist alongside Mike Bailey and Todd Giles for former Hague based outfit ‘The Cannonball String Band’.
