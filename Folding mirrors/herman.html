Herman van Rijn – banjo ukulele

Herman van Rijn played for twenty years as percussionist and singer in The Cajun Company (1993-2013), with whom he recorded three CDs, the first ‘Testing One-One’ recorded in Hilversum The Netherlands, the second ‘La robe de Rosalie’ in Crowly Louisiana, and the third ‘La prairie Ronde’ in Dirk Powell’s Cyprus House studio. He has played all over Europe as well as a great deal in Louisiana at festivals, restaurants and workshops in the Bajou.
