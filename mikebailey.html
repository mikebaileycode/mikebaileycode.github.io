Mike Bailey – fiddle and vocals

Mike Bailey is an oldtime fiddler based in the Hague. He has played gigs and festivals all over the Netherlands (European World of Bluegrass/Pijnackerplein Bluegrass Festival/Embassy Festival/Big Texas BBQ) with former Hague based outfit ‘The Cannonball String Band’. He is also former fiddler and singer for Hague based bands ‘The Dust Bowl Drifters’ and ‘The Bootlegger’s Trial’.
